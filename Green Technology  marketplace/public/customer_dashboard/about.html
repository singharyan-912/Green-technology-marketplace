The GreenTech Story

Founded in 2025, GreenTech started with a simple question: "Why is it so hard to find reliable solar tech and eco-products in one place?"

What began as a small project to curate EV batteries and solar lights has grown into a global marketplace. We partner with vetted vendors who share our passion for the environment, ensuring that every product you buy contributes to a healthier earth.
