# Project Overview

The Green Technology Marketplace** is an online platform designed to promote eco-friendly products by connecting vendors who sell green technology with customers who want to make sustainable purchases.

The platform allows customers to browse and purchase environmentally friendly products, vendors to manage their product listings and orders, and administrators to oversee users, products, and system settings.

This project aims to encourage **sustainable shopping** while providing an organized marketplace for green technology solutions.

## Objectives

* Promote environmentally friendly products.
* Provide a marketplace for vendors selling green technologies.
* Enable customers to easily browse, purchase, and review products.
* Allow administrators to manage users, products, and system settings.

##  User Roles

1) Admin

Admin users have full control over the platform.

**Responsibilities**

* Manage user accounts and roles
* Approve or reject vendor product listings
* Monitor orders and reviews
* Manage system configuration settings

 2) Vendor

Vendors sell green technology products on the platform.

**Responsibilities**

* Add and manage product listings
* Manage customer orders
* Respond to customer reviews
* View sales reports and revenue statistics

3) Customer

Customers can explore and purchase green technology products.

**Responsibilities**

* Browse available products
* Purchase products
* Leave product reviews
* Manage wishlist and profile

##  Core Features

 Admin Functionalities

* **User Management**

  * Create, update, or delete user accounts
  * Assign roles (Admin, Vendor, Customer)

* **Product Listings Management**

  * Approve or reject vendor product submissions

* **System Settings**

  * Manage global configuration settings

* **Order Monitoring**

  * View and track platform orders

* **Review Overview**

  * Monitor customer feedback

---

 Vendor Functionalities

* **Product Listing**

  * Add product name, description, and price
  * Manage existing products

* **Order Management**

  * View incoming orders
  * Update order status

* **Review Responses**

  * Respond to customer feedback

* **Sales Reports**

  * Track revenue and sales performance

* **Profile Management**

  * Update vendor account information

---

###  Customer Functionalities

* **Browse Products**

  * Search and filter green technology products

* **Purchase Products**

  * Add items to cart and complete payment

* **Leave Reviews**

  * Rate products and write comments

* **Wishlist**

  * Save favorite products

* **Order History**

  * View past orders and status

* **Profile Management**

  * Update personal information
